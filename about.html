ABOUT US

The Association of International Students in Azerbaijan is a non- political student organization representing the interests of all international students enrolled in the University. Its aims and objectives are to promote and assist the welfare, social, educational and cultural activities of international students and to promote goodwill and understanding amongst international students.

We are a student-led association that supports and connects international students from a wide variety of countries studying in Azerbaijan. We are dedicated to promoting diversity and to establishing international relationships with the international student community. We intend to organize events and cultural experiences with the help of sponsors that not only engage the international student community but the host community as well.

Azerbaijan is being left out in the world student platform. We seek to unite all foreign students in Baku under the umbrella of the Association of International Students in Azerbaijan. We will present new suggestions about student policy to appropriate bodies, create close relations with international and regional student organizations.
